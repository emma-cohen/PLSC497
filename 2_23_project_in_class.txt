What research question do you want to answer?
Does the framing of "fake news" affect people's beliefs/polarization?

What are two possible answers?
There could be no effect or framing something as "fake news" could make people less likely to believe it.

What are the documents of text data you will analyze?
Tweets of members of Congress

Give two examples of documents in the corpus that you expect will be high in similarity to each other? Two examples that are *low* in similarity?
Tweets from members in the same party are likely to be high in similarity. Tweets from members of different parties will likely be low in similarity.
